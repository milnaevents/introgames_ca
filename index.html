Word Connections

Say a word that relates or connects to the previous word. Rotate clockwise, taking turns saying one word.

Rules

  1. Word must be related to previous word (and can be unrelated to first word)
  2. Cannot repeat word during same sequence
  3. Max 10 seconds per response
If any rule is broken, start again with a new word by pressing Next.

Example
Balloon -> Red -> Stop -> Sign -> Rubber (Rubber is not related to Sign)

Overkill

List every item you can think of that fits the category, without repeating. Take turns rotating clockwise.


Rules

Say without Saying

Your goal is to get the other person to guess the secret word without saying it directly.

Rules

  1. Do not show your screen to others
  2. One hint at a time, one guess per hint
  3. Max 5 hints and 5 guesses
Example Secret Word: Pizza
  • 1-word hint: slice
  • 2-word hint: slice, cheese
  • 3-word hint: slice, cheese, sauce
  • 4-word hint: slice, cheese, sauce, toppings
  • 5-word hint: slice, cheese, sauce, toppings, crust
When secret word has been guessed or 5 attempts completed, switch roles, and start again by pressing Next.

No English

Game Setup

  • Pick a language other than English
  • Start the timer
  • Start a conversation, perhaps asking questions to one another
Rules
  1. Max 5 seconds to think
  2. First person to use an English word is out
  3. Exception: Other person must know correct translation. If translation not known, game continues. Example: Computer may not have a translation.
Continue the contest until someone breaks a rule!
